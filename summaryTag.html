Rabindranath Tagore (1861–1941)
  • Nobel Laureate (1913, Gitanjali).
  • Poet, novelist, short story writer, dramatist, philosopher.
  • Notable works: Gitanjali, Gora, Kabuliwala, Chokher Bali.
  • Likhechilen Bangladesh o India’r national anthem.
Kazi Nazrul Islam (1899–1976)
  • Known as Bidrohi Kobi (Rebel Poet).
  • National poet of Bangladesh.
  • Notable works: Bidrohi, Agnibeena, Bandhonhara.
  • Liked to write about revolution, equality, humanity, music.
